Intro

    Logscape is a powerful platform to monitor all levels of your infrastructure and applications. Logscape has custom applications to monitor Web Servers, Databases and custom inhouse .NET and Java applications. Logscape provides realtime analysis and visualization on all parts of your infrastructure. This guide will give an introduction to different areas of enterprise monitoring.

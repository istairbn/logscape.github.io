How to Deploy an App

An App contains related searches and Workspaces. Apps may also run services for collecting data.

  1. Navigate to the Deployment page by Clicking on 'Configure' then choose the 'Deployment' tab.
  2. Click on the Upload button and select the files that you would like to upload. e.g UnixApp-.0.zip
  3. 3. Once the file has been uploaded it will appear in the table below. Find the App then click 'DEPLOY'.The status of the app will change from UNSTINSTALLED to DEPLOYED
  4. Each App has a Home Workspace. e.g The UnixApp has the Unix Home workspace. You can navigate to the other Workspaces included in the App from the Home Workspace.
